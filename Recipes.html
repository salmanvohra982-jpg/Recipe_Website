Dal Tadka

Dal Tadka is a popular Indian dish made with lentils cooked with onions, tomatoes, garlic, and traditional spices, then topped with a flavorful tempering (tadka) of ghee, cumin, mustard seeds, garlic, and red chilies. It's a staple comfort food often served with rice or roti.

Biryani

Chicken Biryani is a flavorful rice dish made with basmati rice, tender chicken, and a blend of aromatic spices like cumin, cardamom, cinnamon, and cloves. Often cooked with fried onions, garlic, and yogurt, this dish is popular in South Asia and comes in several regional variations, each with unique ingredients and preparation methods.

Recipe From Mother

Origin: Gujarat India

Chicken Curry

Chicken Curry is a popular dish made by cooking chicken in a flavorful gravy of tomatoes, onions, garlic, ginger, and a variety of spices such as turmeric, cumin, coriander, and garam masala. The dish is known for its rich, savory, and slightly spicy flavors. It is commonly served with rice, bread, or naan, and is a favorite in many regions of South Asia and around the world.

Recipe From Sister

Origin: Anand Gujarat
