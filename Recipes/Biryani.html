Chicken Biryani

View All Dishes

What is Biryani?

Chicken Biryani is a flavorful rice dish made with basmati rice, tender chicken, and a blend of aromatic spices like cumin, cardamom, cinnamon, and cloves. Often cooked with fried onions, garlic, and yogurt, this dish is popular in South Asia and comes in several regional variations, each with unique ingredients and preparation methods.

Origin of Biryani

Chicken Biryani is believed to have been influenced by Persian, Afghan, and Mughal culinary traditions. It is thought to have originated in the kitchens of the Mughal Empire, where it was refined with local spices. Over time, it became a beloved dish across South Asia.

How to make Chicken Biryani:

Ingredients:

  • 500g Chicken (with bone preferred)
  • 1 cup Yogurt
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • Salt to taste

For Rice

  • 2 cups Basmati Rice
  • 4-5 Cloves
  • 2-3 Cardamom pods
  • 1 Cinnamon stick
  • 1 Bay Leaf
  • Salt to taste

For Biryani Layering

  • 1 large Onion (sliced and fried until golden brown)
  • Fresh Coriander and Mint Leaves
  • Saffron soaked in warm milk (optional)
  • 2 tbsp Ghee or Oil

Preparation:

  • Marinate chicken with all marinade ingredients for at least 1 hour (overnight preferred).
  • Wash and soak basmati rice for 30 minutes.
  • Fry sliced onions until golden brown; set aside for layering.

Instructions:

Cooking Chicken

  1. In a deep pan, heat oil/ghee and add marinated chicken.
  2. Cook until chicken is tender and the marinade is thick.

Cooking Rice

  1. Boil water with whole spices and salt.
  2. Add soaked rice and cook until 70-80% done. Drain and keep aside.

Layering and Final Cooking

  1. In a pot, layer half of the cooked chicken, then half of the rice.
  2. Top with fried onions, mint, coriander, and saffron milk (if using).
  3. Repeat the layers and finish with the remaining toppings.
  4. Cover the pot tightly with a lid or foil and cook on low heat (dum) for 20 minutes.
  5. Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving. Gently mix before serving.
